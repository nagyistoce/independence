About iNdependence

iNdependence is a Cocoa-based application for Mac OS X which provides an easy-to-use interface for jailbreak, activation, SSH installation, and ringtone/wallpaper/application installation on your iPhone.

Platforms and configurations known to work with iNdependence:

Intel Mac: Mac OS X 10.4.10 - Firmware 1.0, 1.0.1, 1.0.2 - iTunes 7.3 to 7.4.2
PPC Mac: Mac OS X 10.4.10 - Firmware 1.0, 1.0.1, 1.0.2 - iTunes 7.3 to 7.4.2
